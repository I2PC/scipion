Particle validation


This page allows you to validate particles online. The generation of a phantom reconstruction from aligned noise is here presented as a baseline to assess the validity of a reconstruction from micrograph images of presumably identical particles. This is both a sanity check for the microscopist at an early stage in a project, as well as for reconstructions already deposited in the EMDB. So, based on the results, with the reconstruction of aligned gaussian noise, the validity of the reconstruction from the micrograph images can be assessed. Practically, if the resolution of the reconstruction based on the images is not considerably different from the one based on the aligned gaussian noise (for less number of particles), the images may not produce a valid reconstruction (J. Bernard Heymann, 2015).
